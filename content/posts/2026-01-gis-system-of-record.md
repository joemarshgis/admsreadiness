---# GIS as the System of Record for ADMS and OMS

Utilities rarely fail ADMS or OMS projects because of software.
They fail because foundational GIS data isn’t ready.

## Why GIS Is Central
GIS defines:
- Network connectivity
- Phase modeling
- Operating voltage
- Asset relationships

Every downstream system depends on this accuracy.

## Common Data Gaps
- Self-intersecting lines
- Missing or invalid phase designation
- Null facility IDs
- Inconsistent feeder modeling

## What Actually Works
A targeted GIS data readiness assessment
*before* OMS or ADMS implementation or upgrades.
